# Roadmap: FinAlly — AI Trading Workstation

## Overview

FinAlly is a single-Docker-command Bloomberg-style AI trading workstation: live SSE price streaming, a simulated $10k portfolio with instant-fill market orders, and an LLM copilot that can analyze positions and execute trades via structured-output auto-execution. v1.0 (MVP) shipped 2026-04-28 with 11 phases / 51 plans complete and a canonical Playwright harness exiting 0 reproducibly with 21/21 green × 2 consecutive runs.
